Generative-AI-Business-Integration

 Smarter Work, Safer AI (CSIT205)

 Overview
Group project exploring how **Generative AI** can be integrated into business practices.  
Covers industry case studies, strategic recommendations, UN SDG alignment, and ethical considerations.


 Objectives
- Analyze impact of Generative AI across industries (fashion, retail, healthcare).
- Provide strategic roadmap for safe adoption.
- Link AI adoption to UN SDGs (Decent Work, Responsible Consumption).
- Address data management, privacy, and ethics.


 Tools & References
- Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT (idea generation, proofreading)
- Gartner, McKinsey, IBM, WEF reports
- Harvard Business Review, UN SDG documentation



 My Contributions
- Co‑authored **strategic recommendations** section (AI governance, phased roadmap).
- Wrote **UN SDG analysis** (Goal 8 & Goal 12).
- Proofread and structured report sections for clarity.



 Key Case Studies
- **Amarra (Fashion)** → AI‑driven design & personalization.  
- **Starbucks (Retail)** → Deep Brew AI for recommendations & R&D.  
- **Walgreens (Healthcare)** → Chatbots for customer support.



 Outcomes
- Proposed **AI Strategy & Governance Office**.  
- Recommended **Human‑in‑the‑Loop** verification.  
- Highlighted **AI literacy programs** to close skills gap.
